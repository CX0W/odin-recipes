Pasta

Description

Ingredients

For cooking pasta

  • 2 ltr water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 cup pasta

For pasta sauce

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 chilli, slit
  • 1 inch ginger, chopped
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 2 cup tomato puree
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ cup pasta boiled water

Steps

  1. firstly, in a large vessel take 2 litre water and 1 tbsp salt.
  2. get the water to a rolling boil. once the water comes to a boil, add 2 cup pasta.
  3. boil for 7 minutes or until the pasta is al dente. make sure to check the cooking time on package instructions.
  4. crain off the pasta and rinse with cold water to stop from cooking further. keep aside.
  5. to prepare pasta sauce, in a large wok heat 2 tbsp olive oil and 1 tbsp butter.
  6. add 2 clove garlic, 1 chilli and 1 inch ginger. saute slightly.
    7. also, saute ½ onion until the onions shrink slightly.
  7. keeping the flame on low, add 1 tsp chilli powder and ½ tsp turmeric.
  8. saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
  9. further, add 2 cup tomato puree and cook well.
  10. cook until the mixture thickens and oil separates.
  11. also add ½ tsp coriander powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp garam masala and ½ tsp salt.
  12. saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
  13. add 2 tbsp sweet corn, 2 tbsp carrot and 2 tbsp capsicum. cook for a minute.
  14. now add ½ cup pasta boiled water, 2 tbsp tomato sauce, 1 tsp mixed herbs and 1 tsp chilli flakes.
  15. cook until the spices are well combined.
  16. further, add ½ cup cheese and mix well. cheesy pasta sauce is ready.
  17. now add boiled pasta and mix well making sure the pasta sauce is well combined with pasta.
  18. finally, enjoy desi masala pasta topped with cheese and coriander
